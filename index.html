Arun Srivatsan Rangaprasad

I graduated in May 2018 with a Ph.D. from Biorobotics Laboratory in the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University. I was advised by Alonzo Kelly.

For my thesis, I built Lidar simulators for mobile robots, operating in indoor and off-road environments. The simulators were constructed using data-driven methods, and validated for application development on complex real-world scenes.
